About Event

Welcome to Space Land, a global music event in the heart of Stockholm, Sweden. The event has been created by the internationally acclaimed Diana Barahona Olivares and promises to offer a unique experience of music, culture, and creativity.

Space Land is a place where music takes center stage. Here, different genres and cultures come together to create an electrifying atmosphere. From the latest music trends to iconic, timeless sounds – this is a weekend that promises to touch every music lover on a deep level. With passion and creativity as the driving force, artists from around the world will perform, offering a unique sonic journey that takes the audience to new musical heights. Space Land is more than just a concert – it’s a music celebration where every note, every rhythm, and every melody creates a sense of unity and joy.

Welcome to Space Land – where the music is not just heard, it is felt.
